# Exploratory Data Analysis Report

## Objective

To uncover patterns, trends, and relationships within the sales dataset and derive business insights.

## Dataset Overview

* Total Records: 1000
* Total Features: 16 (after feature engineering)

## Descriptive Analysis

### Customer Demographics

* Majority of customers belong to the Adult and Young Adult age groups.
* Gender distribution is balanced.

### Sales Performance

* Certain categories contribute significantly more revenue than others.
* Revenue is concentrated among a few top-performing products.

## SQL Insights

### Top Products

The top 5 products contribute a major share of overall revenue.

### Revenue by City

A small number of cities generate the majority of sales.

### Monthly Trend

Revenue fluctuates across months, indicating seasonality.

## Correlation Analysis

### Quantity vs Sales

A strong positive relationship exists between quantity sold and total sales.

### Age vs Sales

Customer age shows limited correlation with spending.

## Recommendations

1. Focus marketing efforts on high-revenue cities.
2. Increase inventory for top-performing products.
3. Introduce targeted campaigns for high-value customer segments.
4. Monitor monthly trends for seasonal planning.

## Conclusion

The dataset reveals significant opportunities for improving revenue through targeted marketing, inventory optimization, and customer segmentation.
